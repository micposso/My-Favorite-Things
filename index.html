The World of Tacos

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish composed of a corn or wheat tortilla folded or rolled around a filling. The filling can vary widely and includes ingredients such as beef, pork, chicken, seafood, beans, vegetables, and cheese, allowing for great versatility and variety. Tacos are often garnished with various condiments, such as salsa, guacamole, or sour cream, and topped with diced vegetables like onions and cilantro. They are typically eaten without utensils and are known for their flavorful combinations and convenient hand-held form.
